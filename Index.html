20th Century Classic Debuts

The 1970s

Carole King - Writer (1971)

Carole King's first solo album, showcasing her songwriting talents.

The 1980s

Whitney Houston - Whitney Houston (1985)

Whitney Houston's debut stunned the world with her extraordinary vocal prowess.

Tracy Chapman - Tracy Chapman (1988)

A folk-inspired debut that addressed social issues and launched Chapman's career.

21st Century Outstanding Debuts

The 2000s

Norah Jones - Come Away with Me (2002)

A jazz-influenced debut that earned Jones multiple Grammy Awards.

50 Cent - Get Rich or Die Tryin' (2003)

This album catapulted 50 Cent to stardom in the rap world.

Lady Gaga - The Fame (2008)

Lady Gaga shook the music industry with her unique style and image.

Adele - 19 (2008)

Adele's album demonstrated her musical depth and achieved massive success.

The 2010s

Frank Ocean - Channel Orange (2012)

Frank Ocean's debut studio album brought fresh energy to R&B.

SZA - Ctrl (2017)

SZA showcased her distinctive style and voice with this debut.

Billie Eilish - When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? (2019)

Young Billie Eilish quickly became a pop sensation with this album.
